A structured way to evaluate a cloud provider's security posture across five signals -
Visibility, Identity Granularity, Supply Chain, Org Maturity, and Transparency - plus two
cross-cutting lenses: networking, and the _agentic amplifier_ (what changes when an agent,
not a human, is holding the credentials).

Packaged as a Claude skill, it compresses a week-long manual audit into roughly a
40-minute pass and emits per-signal CSV deep-dives, a cross-platform comparison matrix, and
a written report. The thesis: neocloud problems are cloud problems - name the gaps, build
the bridges, hold the line.
